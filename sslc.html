List of scholarships for SSLC students!!


S.NO. SCHOLRSHIP NAME ELIGIBILIY CRITERIA
1 Shiksha Sahyog Scholarship for 10th Students
  • The applicant must be a student of class 10th.
  • The annual family income of the applicant must be less than INR 3,00,000.
2 Aryabhatta National Maths Competition
  • Applicant age between 10 to 24 years.
  • The applicant must be an Indian Resident.
3 Sterling and Wilson Solar Scholarship for students pursuing fulltime ITI
  • Candidates with a minimum of 35% marks in class 10th can apply.
  • Applicants from all genders can apply.
4 ALLEN Champ 2021
  • Candidate studying class 3rd to 10th can participate.
  • To be shortlisted for the final round of activity resulting in ACR 1 to 10, it is mandatory for the students to have at least one achievement.
5 All India Scholarship Test Exam (AISTE)
  • Students of class 1st to 12th class can apply.
6 GSB Scholarship
  • The candidate must belong to the Gaud Saraswat Brahmin community.
  • Students of class 5th to undergraduate courses will be eligible to avail this scholarship.
7 Vidyadhan Scholarship
  • The applicant must pass the 10th examination from the states of Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat and Andhra Pradesh.
  • Parents' income should not be more than 2 lacs per annum.
8 International General Knowledge Olympiad
  • The applicant must be a student of class 10th.
  • The applicant must be an Indian Resident.
9 Karanataka Government Scholarship for OBC Students
  • The candidate must belong to OBC Category.
  • The student must be a domicile of Karnataka.
