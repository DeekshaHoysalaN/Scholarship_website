LIST OF SCHOLARSHIPS FOR ENGINEERING STUDENTS


S.NO. SCHOLRSHIP NAME ELIGIBILIY CRITERIA
1 Post Matric Scholarships Scheme for Minorities
  • Scholarship will be awarded to the students who have secured not less than 50% marks or equivalent grade in the previous final examination.
  • The annual income of whose parents/guardians from all sources does not exceed Rs. 2.50 lakh.
2 Merit Cum Means Scholarship for Professional and Technical Courses CS (Minorities)
  • Student should have passed Class X Examination from the CBSE and secured 60% or more.
3 Scholarships for Top Class Education for Students with Disabilities
  • Students who are specially abled.
  • All under graduate, post graduate and diplomo students can apply.
4 Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme for Central Armed Police Forces and Assam Rifles
  • Having a minimum 60% marks in Minimum Entry Qualification (MEQ) i.e. 10+2/Diploma/Graduation or equivalent in case of a new applicant.
  • For applicants applying under renewal category, it is mandatory to pass each subsequent academic year of the professional courses being pursued by them with minimum 50% marks.
5 Post Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities
  • Parent's income must be less than 2.5 lakhs.
6 Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students
  • Candidates who are above the 80th percentile of successful students who have passed class 12 Pursuing regular UG/PG course.
  • Annual family income less than Rs.8 Lakh
7 Financial Assistance for Education of the Wards of Beedi/Cine/IOMC/LSDM Workers
  • Students whose parents are either Beedi, Iron Ore Manganese & Chrome Ore Mines, Limestone and Dolomite Mines, Cine worker with at least 6 months of service can apply for this scheme.
