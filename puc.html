List of scholarships for PUC students!!


S.NO. SCHOLRSHIP NAME ELIGIBILIY CRITERIA
1 NSDL Shiksha Sahyog Scholarship for 11th Students
  • The applicant must be a student of class 11th.
  • The annual family income of the applicant must be less than INR 3,00,000.
2 National Merit Cum Means Scholarship (NMMS)
  • Students Parents Annual Family income should be less than 1,50,000 per annum.
  • They should have done their complete schooling in State Govt, Govt aided and Local Body Schools.
3 MOMA Scholarship Merit cum Means (MCM)
  • A candidate must of the minority class.
  • A Candidate must be residing in India.
4 Gaud Saraswat Brahman Scholarship (GSB) 2022
  • Students must belong to the Gaud Saraswat Brahmin community.
  • Students of class 5th to undergraduate courses will be eligible for availing of this scholarship.
5 All India Scholarship Test Exam (AISTE)
  • Students of class 1st to 12th class can apply.
6 Vidyasiri Scholarship for 10th passed students
  • The candidate must belong to SC/ ST/ OBC/ PWD community.
  • Candidates must be domicile holders of Karnataka with a family income should be 1 lakh to 2 lakh P/A or less.
7 Siksha Abhiyan scholarship for 10th passed students
  • Students from class 8th to class 12th are eligible to apply for this scheme.
  • Students should be a resident of India.
8 Vidyadhan Scholarship for 10th passed students
  • Students who have completed their 10th with a minimum of 90% marks from Karnataka, Telangana, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Gujarat can apply.
  • Their family income should not be more than 2 lakh P A.
9 Merit cum Means Scholarship for Minorities, Karnataka
  • Applicants must belong to a minority community as notified by the Government of India.
  • The candidates must be studying in class 11 or above.
